Essay

The centered column still wins for reading.

By A. Writer · 6 min read · Updated today

Introduction

When the job is reading, constrain the line length and get out of the way. A single centered column with a quiet top bar is the most durable layout on the web — it is what docs, blogs, and essays converge on for a reason.

Why centered

Global navigation lives in the sticky header so it is always one reach away. Local navigation — the structure of this page — lives in the in-page rail, which tracks where you are as you scroll.

The reader should never wonder where they are or how far is left.

The measure

Hold the body to roughly 60–75 characters per line. Everything else — type scale, spacing, rhythm — follows from protecting that measure.

Closing

Fork this when the deliverable is words. Swap the serif for a sans if the voice calls for it; the structure does not change.
