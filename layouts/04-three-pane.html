Re: the Q3 roadmap

Maya Chen · to me · 9:24 AM

This is the detail pane. In a three-pane layout the rail picks the context, the middle list is the index, and this pane renders whatever is selected — a message, a file, a record, a chat thread.

The pattern shines when users move quickly between many similar items and want the list to stay put. Keyboard nav (j/k, enter) fits naturally here.

On narrow screens the three panes collapse to a drill-in: tap a list item and this pane slides over it, with a back affordance to return.
