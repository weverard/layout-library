Accordion spine

One open at a time.

01

The spine is the navigation

+

A column of titles you can scan top to bottom. Clicking a header expands its content in place and closes whatever was open — so the page never gets overwhelming.

Best when sections are peers and readers want to dip into one without losing the map of the whole.

02

Cheap to scan, cheap to commit

+

Headers stay compact so the table of contents is always visible. Opening one is a small, reversible commitment — no page load, no scroll jump.

03

Great for FAQs and process

+

Questions, steps, features, modules — anything that's a list of self-contained sections fits the spine. Deep-link by opening the matching item.

04

Animate the height, not the opacity

+

A max-height transition gives the satisfying push-down that signals the content belongs to this header. Rotate the plus into a cross to confirm state.
